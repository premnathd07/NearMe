Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS) is a deemed university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. It encompasses a wide range of institutions, including medical, dental, engineering, management, and allied health sciences colleges. Known for its strong focus on research, innovation, and interdisciplinary education, SIMATS offers modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and high-quality teaching hospitals. The institution emphasizes practical learning, academic excellence, and holistic student development, making it one of the well-recognized educational hubs in South India.
